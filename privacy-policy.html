1. Overview

Sumtastic: Math Puzzle Game is a mobile game designed for entertainment. We aim to collect as little information as reasonably possible. However, because the App may rely on third-party platform and advertising services, certain device or usage-related information may be processed automatically when the App is used.

2. Information We May Process

Depending on your device, region, app version, and enabled services, the following categories of data may be processed:

  • Device and app information: device model, operating system version, app version, language, and general technical identifiers.
  • Diagnostics and performance data: crash logs, stability information, and technical diagnostics used to improve reliability.
  • Advertising-related identifiers and signals: advertising ID (where available), app set ID, and related ad delivery data.
  • Network and approximate location signals: IP address, coarse location inferred from IP or device signals, and connection-related information.
  • Ad interaction information: information related to ad impressions, rewarded ads, clicks, and fraud prevention.
  • Locally stored game data: settings, progress, and similar gameplay preferences may be stored on your device to operate the App properly.

3. How This Information Is Used

  • To operate and maintain the App.
  • To provide and manage advertising, including rewarded ads where available.
  • To detect bugs, crashes, abuse, invalid traffic, and technical issues.
  • To improve app stability, performance, and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and platform requirements.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services that process data under their own terms and privacy policies, including:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Advertising Technologies
  • Google Play Data Safety Information

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be processed or shared with service providers and platform partners, such as Google Play Services and Google AdMob, to deliver app functionality, advertising, diagnostics, measurement, and fraud prevention.

6. Data Retention

Locally stored game data generally remains on your device until you delete the App or clear its storage. Data processed by third-party services is retained according to those providers’ own policies and legal obligations.

7. Your Choices

  • You can reset or delete your Android advertising ID in your device privacy settings, where supported.
  • You can manage ad personalization settings through your Google or device settings, where available.
  • You can clear local app data or uninstall the App to remove data stored on your device.

8. Children’s Privacy

Sumtastic: Math Puzzle Game is intended for a general audience unless otherwise specified on its store listing. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us directly, please contact us so we can review the situation.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to support the security of the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure. Third-party providers may apply their own security measures, including encryption in transit, according to their documentation and practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect app updates, legal requirements, or changes in data practices. The updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised “Last Updated” date.
